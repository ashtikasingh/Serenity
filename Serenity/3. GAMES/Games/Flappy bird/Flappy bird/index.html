Flappy Bird

How To Play

  1. Start the game by clicking anywhere in the canvas.
  2. Click to make the bird flap and dodge the pipe.
  3. Stay in the middle of screen until the first set of pipes appears.
  4. If you hit a pipe or the ground, the game ends.
  5. Find your rhythm for higher and lower pipes.
  6. Now play and increase you score.


Medals

Up till 40, every ten pipes you pass you will earn a medal ranging from bronze to platinum. These do not affect the gameplay, but they are an achievement to earn:

  • 10 pipes- Bronze Medal
  • 20 pipes- Silver Medal
  • 30 pipes- Gold Medal
  • 40+ pipes- Platinum Medal

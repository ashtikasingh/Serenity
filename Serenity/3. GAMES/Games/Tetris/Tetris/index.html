How To Play

  1. Start the game on the zero score.
  2. Move the tetrominoes left and right with the arrow keys buttons.
  3. Understand the different tetrominoes and arrange according to it.
  4. Use the up button to rotate the tetrominoes to fit holes.
  5. Complete lines to gain points and raise the score.
  6. Check upcoming pieces and plan for them.

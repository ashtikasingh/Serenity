Yoga Experts

Brahmakumari Shivani

Brahamakumari Shivani is a spiritual thinker and motivator who has been under the influence of spiritual and religious teachings since her childhood. She is a gold medalist in Electronics Engineering from Pune University. She worked in the production team of Brahmakumaris television presentations in Delhi where senior teachers used to record teachings. In 2007 she was asked to handle the queries of viewers due to unavailability of teachers. This led to a new television show in 2007 Awakening with Brahmakumaris where she used to be interviewed by the host. This brought many viewers drawn towards her wisdom, simplicity and grace. Her TV series with Bollywood actor Suresh Oberoi has been adapted in the book: Happiness Unlimited. She has been a rajayoga teacher from Prajapita Brahma Kumaris Ishwariya Vishwa Vidyalaya, Mount Abu.

Deepika Mehta

Three words aptly describe Deepika - Beauty, Balance and Brilliance. A strong willed person who overcame her life threatening injuries through yoga and meditation. She underwent training at Sivananda Yoga Kendra, Kerala and later through K. Pattabhi Jois in 2002. Deepika has gained wisdom of yoga from renowned yoga practitioners from around the world. She has trained famous Indian celebrities and has her own TV show Yoga City. She is Authorized level 2 Ashtanga Yoga teacher by KPJAYI.

Swami Govindananda

A Charismatic teacher and a skilled yoga expert, Swami Govindananda has accumulated wealth of knowledge from various yoga schools of repute. His guru was Swami Vishnudevananda. Swamiji joined Sivananda Yoga ashram in Kerala and acquired yoga knowledge from International Sivananda Yoga Vedanta Centre organization. He graduated in physics and a B.Ed allowing him to impart complicated concepts with ease which is easily understood by students irrespective of their age or culture. He teaches his students involving the elements of passion, energy and humour which is inexhaustible. With over 30 years of teaching experience Swamiji has dedicated 23 years in Sivananda yoga Vedanta centers existing worldwide. He teaches a wide range of subjects from traditional yoga forms- Hatha, Raja yoga, Meditation, philosophy, Vedanta philosophy, Sanskrit, Scriptures, Pranayama, yogic diet to advanced yoga and teaching skills.

Nivedita Joshi

Having gone through severe spine injuries, destiny took Nivedita in the care of B.K.S. Iyengar. With her tenacity and optimism she managed to conquer her physical limitation and after a year walked out of the Iyengar Institute fully transformed both physically and spiritually. Since then Nivedita has been a dedicated trainer and practitioner of Iyengar yoga. Her positivity and sense of social responsibility is transforming lives all across the country and different parts of the world. Currently she is the head of Iyengar yoga centre- Yogakshema, New Delhi. Nivedita has been and continues to be an active participant and trainer in workshops and conventions organized for corporates and people in general both national and international.

Dr. S.N. Omkar

Dr. S.N. Omkar founded Yoga Mandir in 1989 in order to promote holistic health through yoga. The main emphasis of the center is to promote yoga education amongst children to ensure their overall development. Engineer by profession, Omkar has done B.E.( Mechanical),MSc. in Engineering and PhD in Aerospace Engineering. He acquired yoga training from renowned Yogacharya Dr. B.K.S. Iyengar. He is working as Chief Research Scientist at Indian Institute of Science, Dept of Aerospace Engineering. Dr. S.N. Omkar was appointed as Yoga coach for Karnataka cricket team and has trained renowned cricketers like Sachin Tendulkar and M.S. Dhoni. Anil Kumble is one of the trustees of his yoga center- Yoga Mandir. Dr. Omkar has many recognitions and honours to his credit like Editoral Board Member of Journal of yoga and Physical Therapy. He has conducted numerous events on yoga to serve the unprivileged groups of our society like yoga for disabled people. For the first time yoga was introduced for cricket team under his guidance. Dr. Omkar has been a part of many national and international conventions on yoga. He has inspired people through his radio and television shows. He conducted yoga lectures for astronauts and also introduced SURYA program- Student Upliftment and Rejuvenation through Yoga.
